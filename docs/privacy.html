Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

CartoonMe is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Important: CartoonMe processes all photos 100% locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers or any third party.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The only information processed by CartoonMe:

  • Photos you select: Used solely for image processing within the app. Photos are processed on-device and never leave your phone.
  • Processed images: Saved to your device's photo library at your request.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Location information
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Any photos except those you explicitly select

4. Data Processing

All AI model processing happens locally on your device:

  • Photos are not uploaded to any server
  • Photos are not sent to third parties
  • All AI processing runs on your device using CoreML
  • We cannot access your photos

5. Photo Library Access

CartoonMe requires photo library access for:

  • Read access: To select photos you want to process
  • Write access: To save processed images to your photo library

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device's Settings app.

6. Third-Party Services

CartoonMe does not use any third-party analytics or data collection services. All features work completely offline.

7. Children's Privacy

CartoonMe is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.
